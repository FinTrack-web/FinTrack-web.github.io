Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mission, technology, and how you can help.

How are sharks tracked?

We use satellite transmitters attached to the shark's dorsal fin. These tags send signals to satellites whenever the shark breaks the surface, allowing us to pinpoint its location.

Is tagging harmful to the sharks?

No, the tagging process is done by trained professionals and is designed to be as minimally invasive as possible. The tags are applied quickly and fall off naturally over time.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation directly funds the purchase of new satellite tags, research expeditions, and educational programs to raise awareness about shark conservation.

How can I get involved besides donating?

You can help by sharing our mission on social media, educating your friends and family, and supporting sustainable seafood practices.

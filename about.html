About SharkTracker

Discover the technology and passion behind our mission to track and protect sharks worldwide.

Our Mission

SharkTracker was founded with a singular purpose: to bridge the gap between scientific research and public awareness about shark conservation. We believe that by making shark tracking data accessible and engaging, we can foster a deeper appreciation for these magnificent creatures and the critical role they play in our oceans.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with marine biology expertise to provide real-time tracking, historical migration patterns, and conservation alerts for various shark species around the globe.

The Technology

Satellite Tracking

We utilize advanced satellite tags that transmit location data in real-time. These tags are specially designed to minimize impact on the sharks while providing accurate positioning information.

Data Analysis

Our machine learning algorithms process millions of data points to identify migration patterns, feeding grounds, and potential threats to shark populations.
